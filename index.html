How I Work

A Refined
Process

Every project begins with listening. Understanding the story, the intent, and the visual language before a single node is placed or cut is made.

01

Creative Brief & Consultation

A detailed conversation about the project's visual references, tone, and delivery requirements. No guessing — clear creative alignment from day one.

02

Media Ingest & Organisation

Your footage is ingested, organised, and colour managed properly. ACES, ARRI LogC, Sony S-Log, RED IPP2 — handled with the correct pipeline.

03

Primary & Secondary Grading

Scene-by-scene work, shot matching, and look application. Each frame receives individual attention before the reel flows as a cohesive whole.

04

Client Review & Revisions

Secure online review links shared for feedback. Revisions handled promptly and professionally until you're satisfied with every frame.

05

Final Delivery

Platform-specific renders, editorial exports, and archive packages delivered via fast transfer. Clean, labelled, and ready for broadcast or upload.
